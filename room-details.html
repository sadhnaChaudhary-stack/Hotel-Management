Deluxe King Room

About This Room

Our Deluxe King Room is a sanctuary of comfort and sophistication. Spanning 45 sq metres on the 12th floor, this beautifully appointed room features a plush king-sized bed dressed in 600-thread-count Egyptian cotton, a private balcony with breathtaking city views, and a marble ensuite bathroom with a deep soaking tub.

Every detail has been carefully curated — from the handcrafted teak furniture to the smart home controls, Nespresso machine, and custom Aromatherapy Associates bath amenities. Perfect for business travellers and couples seeking a luxurious urban retreat.
